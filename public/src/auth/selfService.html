Self Service

This section is intended for self-registration of users who are not already joined to an organization account.


Getting Started

To self-register, you will need to be authenticated with your organization's Microsoft account. Once succesfully authenticated, you will be registered in our system and can proceed to the login page.

Clicking the button below will redirect you to Microsoft then back to Ruptive. Upon account approval, you will receive the role of a "Power User" by default (For "Admin" or "Tenant" level access, you would need to email our helpdesk: helpdesk@ruptive.cx)
